The Privacy Sandbox is Chromium's alternative to traditional targeted advertisement, following their disapproval of the same.

The project plants to replace targeted ads with FloCs, displaying advertisements to groups of multiple users with similar interests. This has been subjected to considerable criticism and apprehension, mainly on part of the advertisers who are concerned that this project might be an attempt at monopolization.

UK's Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) opened an antitrust (formal) investigation into this project. So far, the evaluations seem to be going well.
